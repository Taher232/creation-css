Ursula Burns

Ursula Burns, (born September 20, 1958, New York, New York, U.S.),
American business executive who served as CEO (2009–16) and chairman (2010–17)
of the international document-management and business-services company Xerox Corporation.
She was the first African American woman to serve as CEO of a Fortune 500 company
and the first female to accede to the position of CEO of such a company in succession after another female.
